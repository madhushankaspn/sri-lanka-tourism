About Negombo Beach

Negombo Beach, located just 7 kilometers from Bandaranaike International Airport, is one of Sri Lanka's most accessible and popular beach destinations. Known as "Little Rome" due to its strong Catholic heritage, Negombo offers a unique blend of golden sandy beaches, rich cultural history, and vibrant fishing community.

The beach stretches approximately 8 kilometers along the western coast, featuring calm waters protected by an offshore reef, making it ideal for swimming and water sports. Negombo's fishing heritage is still very much alive, with colorful fishing boats (oruwas) dotting the shore and fresh seafood available daily at the fish market.

Fishing Culture & Traditions

Traditional Oruwa Boats

Colorful outrigger fishing boats that line the beach every morning and evening.

Fish Auction

Daily fish market where the morning's catch is auctioned to restaurants and vendors.

Stilt Fishermen

Traditional method of fishing on stilts, though now mostly for tourist demonstrations.

Location Advantage

Only 15-20 minutes from Colombo Airport. Perfect for first/last night stays in Sri Lanka.
