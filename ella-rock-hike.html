About Ella Rock Hike

Ella Rock is one of Sri Lanka's most popular hiking destinations, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the hill country. The challenging but rewarding trek takes you through tea plantations, eucalyptus forests, and rural villages before reaching the summit at 1,041 meters (3,415 feet) above sea level.

The hike is approximately 8-10 km round trip (5-6 miles) and takes 3-4 hours depending on your pace. The trail can be tricky to navigate, so hiring a local guide is recommended for first-time visitors. The summit offers spectacular views of Ella Gap, Little Adam's Peak, and on clear days, you can see as far as the southern coastal plains.

Recommended Itinerary

1

Start Early (6:00 AM)

Begin your hike early to avoid midday heat and crowds. Catch sunrise from the summit.

2

Railway Track Walk (45 mins)

Walk along the famous Kandy-Badulla railway line from Ella station.

3

Tea Plantation Trail (1 hour)

Climb through lush tea plantations with local tea pickers.

4

Forest & Summit (1.5 hours)

Trek through eucalyptus forest before the final ascent to the summit.

Hike Essentials

Footwear: Sturdy hiking shoes

Water: 2+ liters per person

Sun Protection: Hat, sunscreen

Clothing: Light layers, rain jacket

Camera: For amazing photos

Guide: Recommended for first-timers

Best Time to Hike

Morning: Start at 6 AM for sunrise (bring flashlight)

Season: December to April (dry season)

Avoid: Heavy rain days (trail gets slippery)
