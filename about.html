About Discover Sri Lanka

Your trusted guide to exploring the beautiful island nation of Sri Lanka

Our Mission

At Discover Sri Lanka, our mission is to showcase the incredible beauty, rich culture, and diverse experiences that Sri Lanka has to offer to travelers from around the world. We believe in promoting sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while preserving the natural and cultural heritage of this magnificent island.

Founded in 2010, we've helped over 500,000 travelers plan their perfect Sri Lankan adventure, from solo backpackers to family vacations and luxury retreats.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Sustainable Tourism

We promote eco-friendly practices and support conservation efforts to protect Sri Lanka's natural beauty for future generations.

Community Support

We work with local communities, providing fair employment and supporting small businesses across the island.

Authentic Experiences

We focus on genuine cultural exchanges and authentic experiences that respect local traditions and heritage.

Traveler Safety

Your safety is our priority. We provide up-to-date travel advice and work with trusted local partners.
